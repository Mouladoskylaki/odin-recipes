Torsk (Scandinavian Cod)

Torsk is a Scandinavian dish of poached and broiled cod with butter. This recipe uses frozen fish; defrosting is not necessary. Be sure to use butter, as there is too much water in margarine.

Ingredients

6 cups water, or more as needed 1 cup white sugar 2 tablespoons salt 6 (6 ounce) fillets cod 1 Â½ cups butter, melted 1 dash paprika

Directions

Preheat the broiler. Lightly grease a cookie sheet. Mix together water, sugar, and salt in a large saucepan. Add fillets to water mixture; add more water to fully submerge fillets if needed. Bring water to a boil over medium-high heat. Boil for 3 to 5 minutes. Remove fillets from water and blot dry on paper towels. Brush with 6 tablespoons melted butter; sprinkle with paprika. Broil in the preheated broiler until fillets are golden brown, 8 to 10 minutes per inch of thickness. Serve with remaining melted butter.
